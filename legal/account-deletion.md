# Account & Data Deletion

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026

This page explains how to delete your **vibi** account and associated data. It applies to the vibi mobile application (iOS bundle `com.vibi.ios`, Android package `com.vibi.app`) and any data linked to your vibi account.

---

## Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended, immediate)

1. Open the vibi app and sign in.
2. Open the **account menu** (tap your profile in the top right).
3. Tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Delete**.

Your account is **permanently deleted** on our servers right away, and you are signed out on the device. This action cannot be undone.

---

## Option 2 — Request by email

If you have uninstalled the app, cannot sign in, or only use the Premiere Pro Plugin, email us from (or stating) the address you signed up with and we will process the deletion after verifying your identity.

**Contact:** [jepark2934@gmail.com](mailto:jepark2934@gmail.com?subject=Account%20Deletion%20Request) — subject "Account Deletion Request".

---

## What is deleted and what is kept

| Data | What happens |
|---|---|
| Account data (email, name, user ID) | Removed within **30 days** of the deletion request |
| Uploaded source media | Deleted within **72 hours** of job completion |
| Separation results kept as history (stems + transcript) | Removed on account deletion, or up to **90 days** |
| Rendered output | Removed on account deletion, or up to **30 days** |
| Credit balance & projects | Removed on account deletion |
| Purchase records | Retained as required by tax/accounting law (typically **5 years**), then deleted |

For full details on how we handle your data, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy).
